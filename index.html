Best Movie Ive seen recently

Oppenheimer

The story behind the father of the atomic bomb, J. Robert Oppenheimer

This movie was very popular at the time of the release,
being released at the same time as Barbie, the movie
had much hype around it. Overall, i'd say it lived up
to much of the hype, as it presented a phenomenal score
along with amazing visuals and sound effects. Although the
movie is quite long, i'd say over all the movie is quite
entertaining and that the director Christopher Nolan did
an excellent job.
